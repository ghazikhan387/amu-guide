## METADATA & GLOSSARY

- **External (EXT):** Students from outside AMU  
- **Internal (INT):** Students from within AMU (e.g., progressing from one AMU programme to another)  
- **Non-Resident:** Day scholars (no hostel)  
- **Resident:** Hostel boarders  
- **SF:** Self-Financing programme  
- **PT:** Part-Time programme  
- **SFS:** Self-Financing Scheme  

Fee columns follow this pattern for every course:  
`Boys Non-Res EXT | Boys Non-Res INT | Boys Res EXT | Boys Res INT | Girls Non-Res EXT | Girls Non-Res INT | Girls Res EXT | Girls Res INT`
