## Scholarships and Financial Aid

Aligarh Muslim University offers various scholarships to support students from different backgrounds. These include university-funded scholarships, government scholarships, and private endowment scholarships.

## University Scholarships

### Merit-Based Scholarships
- **University Merit Scholarship:** Awarded to top-performing students based on academic results each semester.
- **Sir Syed Ahmad Khan Scholarship:** For students demonstrating outstanding academic excellence.
- **Gold Medal Awards:** Each department awards gold medals to the top students at convocation.

### Need-Based Financial Aid
- **Fee Concession:** Partial or full fee waivers for students from economically weaker backgrounds.
- **Student Aid Fund:** Financial assistance for students facing sudden financial hardship.
- **Earn While You Learn Scheme:** Part-time employment opportunities within the university for needy students.

## Government Scholarships

### Central Government Schemes
- **Post-Matric Scholarship for Minorities:** Government of India scholarship for minority community students (administered by the Ministry of Minority Affairs).
- **National Scholarship Portal Schemes:** Various scholarships available through the NSP portal (scholarships.gov.in).
- **INSPIRE Fellowship:** Department of Science and Technology fellowship for science students.
- **UGC Fellowship:** Junior Research Fellowship (JRF) and Senior Research Fellowship (SRF) for research scholars.

### State Government Schemes
- **UP Government Scholarships:** Various schemes by the Uttar Pradesh state government for domicile students.
- **Other State Scholarships:** Students from other states may be eligible for their respective state government scholarships.

## Maulana Azad National Fellowship (MANF)
- Specifically for minority community research scholars pursuing M.Phil./Ph.D.
- Funded by the Ministry of Minority Affairs, Government of India
- Provides monthly fellowship and contingency grant

## Private and Endowment Scholarships
- Several private trusts and alumni associations offer scholarships to AMU students.
- Endowment scholarships established by donors for specific departments or communities.

## How to Apply

1. Check eligibility criteria for the specific scholarship
2. Apply through the AMU scholarship portal or the relevant government portal
3. Submit required documents (income certificate, caste certificate, academic transcripts)
4. Applications are typically invited at the beginning of each academic session
5. Renewal of scholarships requires maintaining a minimum academic standard

## Eligibility Criteria (General)

- Must be a bonafide student of AMU
- Minimum academic performance as specified by each scholarship
- Family income below the specified threshold for need-based scholarships
- Domicile and community certificates as required by government schemes

*For the latest information on available scholarships and application deadlines, visit the AMU website or contact the Proctor's Office.*
