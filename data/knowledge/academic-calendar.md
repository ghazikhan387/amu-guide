## Academic Calendar 2025–26

Aligarh Muslim University follows a semester system for most programs. The academic year is divided into two main semesters with examination periods.

## Semester Structure

### Odd Semester (First Semester)
- **Session Begins:** August 2025
- **Classes Commence:** First/Second week of August 2025
- **Mid-Semester Break:** October 2025 (approximately 1 week during Diwali/Eid holidays)
- **End-Semester Examinations:** November–December 2025
- **Winter Vacation:** Mid-December 2025 to Early January 2026

### Even Semester (Second Semester)
- **Classes Commence:** January 2026
- **Mid-Semester Break:** March 2026 (approximately 1 week during Holi)
- **End-Semester Examinations:** April–May 2026
- **Summer Vacation:** June–July 2026

## Important Academic Dates

- **Admission Process Begins:** January 2026 (for the next academic session)
- **AMU Entrance Examination:** April–June 2026
- **Results Announcement:** June–July 2026
- **New Session Orientation:** August 2026
- **Convocation:** Typically held in March or April

## Examination Schedule

- Internal assessments and assignments are conducted throughout the semester
- End-semester examinations are conducted by the Controller of Examinations
- Practical examinations are held before the theory examinations
- Results are typically declared within 4–6 weeks of examinations

## Public Holidays Observed

AMU observes all national and major religious holidays, including:
- Republic Day (26 January)
- Independence Day (15 August)
- Gandhi Jayanti (2 October)
- Eid-ul-Fitr
- Eid-ul-Adha
- Milad-un-Nabi (Prophet Muhammad's Birthday)
- Diwali
- Holi
- Muharram
- Christmas
- Sir Syed Day (17 October — Founder's Day)

## Academic Rules

- Minimum 75% attendance is required for appearing in examinations
- Students must register for each semester within the prescribed dates
- Late registration may attract a penalty fee
- Re-examination facility is available for students who fail in up to two subjects
- Academic standing is reviewed each semester

*Note: The academic calendar is subject to change. Students should check the official AMU website and departmental notice boards for the latest updates.*
