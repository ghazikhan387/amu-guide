## Frequently Asked Questions

### General

**Q: What is AMU?**
A: Aligarh Muslim University (AMU) is a public central university located in Aligarh, Uttar Pradesh, India. Founded in 1875 by Sir Syed Ahmad Khan as the Muhammadan Anglo-Oriental College, it was granted university status in 1920. It is one of the oldest and most prestigious universities in India.

**Q: Is AMU a government university?**
A: Yes, AMU is a central university funded by the Government of India through the University Grants Commission (UGC). It is established under the Aligarh Muslim University Act of 1920.

**Q: Where is AMU located?**
A: AMU is located in Aligarh city, Uttar Pradesh, India. The nearest major cities are Delhi (approximately 130 km) and Agra (approximately 90 km). Aligarh is well-connected by rail and road.

**Q: How large is the AMU campus?**
A: The AMU campus covers approximately 467.6 hectares (1,155 acres), making it one of the largest university campuses in Asia. It functions as a self-contained township.

### Admissions

**Q: How do I apply for admission to AMU?**
A: Admissions are through the AMU Entrance Examination (AMUEE). Apply online through the official admissions portal (amucontrollerexams.com) when applications open, typically in January–February each year.

**Q: Is the AMU entrance exam online or offline?**
A: The AMU entrance exam is primarily conducted offline (OMR-based) at designated test centres. Some programs may have online components.

**Q: What is the minimum qualification for undergraduate admission?**
A: For most undergraduate programs, candidates must have completed 10+2 (Class 12) or equivalent from a recognized board. Specific subject and percentage requirements vary by program.

**Q: Can students from outside India apply?**
A: Yes, international students can apply through the International Students' Adviser Office. They may apply under self-finance, ICCR scholarship, or government-to-government agreement categories.

**Q: Is there reservation at AMU?**
A: Yes, AMU follows the Government of India's reservation policy, including reservations for SC, ST, OBC (Non-Creamy Layer), EWS, and Persons with Disabilities (PwD).

### Academic Life

**Q: What is the medium of instruction?**
A: The medium of instruction is primarily English for most programs, especially in science, engineering, and medicine. Some arts and theology programs may use Urdu.

**Q: Does AMU follow a semester or annual system?**
A: Most programs at AMU follow the semester system, with two semesters per academic year (odd semester: August–December; even semester: January–May).

**Q: What is the attendance requirement?**
A: Students must maintain a minimum of 75% attendance in each course to be eligible to appear in end-semester examinations.

### Hostel and Campus Life

**Q: Is hostel accommodation compulsory?**
A: AMU is a residential university, and hostel accommodation is strongly encouraged. However, local students may opt for day-scholar status.

**Q: How are hostels allotted?**
A: Hostel allotment is done at the time of admission based on availability. Priority is typically given to outstation students.

**Q: What are the mess (dining) arrangements?**
A: Each hostel has its own mess (dining hall) serving breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Mess fees are paid separately on a monthly basis and are very affordable.

**Q: Is the campus safe?**
A: AMU has a comprehensive security system with guards at all entry/exit points, CCTV surveillance, and a university security force (Proctor's Office). The campus is generally considered safe.

### Fees and Financial Aid

**Q: What are the approximate fees at AMU?**
A: AMU has one of the lowest fee structures among top Indian universities. Undergraduate fees typically range from ₹5,000 to ₹35,000 per semester depending on the program.

**Q: Are scholarships available?**
A: Yes, AMU offers various scholarships including university merit scholarships, government scholarships for minorities, and need-based financial aid. See the Scholarships section for details.

### Infrastructure

**Q: Does AMU have a hospital?**
A: Yes, AMU has the Jawaharlal Nehru Medical College Hospital, a 900+ bed teaching hospital providing comprehensive healthcare services to students, staff, and the public.

**Q: Is there Wi-Fi on campus?**
A: Yes, AMU has campus-wide Wi-Fi connectivity. Individual departments and hostels also have wired internet connections.

**Q: What sports facilities are available?**
A: AMU has excellent sports facilities including cricket grounds, football fields, hockey ground, swimming pool, gymnasium, tennis courts, badminton courts, basketball courts, and more.
