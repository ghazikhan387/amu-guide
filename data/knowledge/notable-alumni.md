## Notable Alumni

Aligarh Muslim University has produced a distinguished array of alumni who have excelled in various fields including politics, academia, science, literature, law, and the arts. Below is a list of some of the most prominent alumni.

## Presidents and Heads of State

- **Dr. Zakir Husain** — Third President of India (1967–1969). He was also the Vice-Chancellor of AMU (1948–1956) and a renowned educationist.
- **Gen. Muhammad Zia-ul-Haq** — Sixth President of Pakistan (1978–1988). Studied at the university before partition.
- **Khwaja Nazimuddin** — Second Governor-General of Pakistan and former Prime Minister of Pakistan.

## Politicians and Diplomats

- **Maulana Muhammad Ali Jauhar** — A prominent leader of the Khilafat Movement and Indian independence movement.
- **Rafi Ahmed Kidwai** — Freedom fighter and Union Minister in independent India.
- **Mukhtar Ahmed Ansari** — President of the Indian National Congress and a renowned surgeon.
- **Nawab Ismail Khan** — Prominent Indian political figure.

## Judiciary

- **Justice Shah Sulaiman** — One of the first Indian Chief Justices of a High Court.
- **Justice Baharul Islam** — Former Judge of the Supreme Court of India.

## Academics and Scientists

- **Sir Ross Masood** — Grandson of Sir Syed Ahmad Khan, educationist, and Vice-Chancellor of AMU.
- **Prof. Mushirul Hasan** — Renowned historian and former Vice-Chancellor of Jamia Millia Islamia.
- **Dr. Salimuzzaman Siddiqui** — Renowned Pakistani chemist known for his research on neem.

## Literature and Arts

- **Ismat Chughtai** — Celebrated Urdu fiction writer, known for her bold literary works.
- **Kaifi Azmi** — Renowned Urdu poet and lyricist for Hindi cinema.
- **Jan Nisar Akhtar** — Prominent Urdu poet.
- **Majrooh Sultanpuri** — Legendary Bollywood lyricist and Urdu poet.
- **Sardar Jafri** — Progressive Urdu poet and writer.
- **Rasheed Jahan** — Pioneering Urdu feminist writer.

## Sports

- **Mansoor Ali Khan Pataudi (Tiger Pataudi)** — Legendary Indian cricketer and former captain of the Indian cricket team.
- **Mushtaq Ali** — One of India's greatest opening batsmen in cricket.

## Film and Entertainment

- **Javed Akhtar** — Acclaimed Bollywood screenwriter, lyricist, and poet.
- **Naseeruddin Shah** — Celebrated Indian film actor and one of the finest actors in Indian cinema.
- **Shabana Azmi** — Award-winning Indian film actress and social activist.

## Business and Industry

- Several AMU alumni have gone on to establish successful businesses and hold leadership positions in major corporations across India and internationally.

## AMU Alumni Association

The AMU Old Boys' Association (AMUOBA) is an active alumni network with chapters across India and internationally. It organises regular events, reunions, and networking opportunities for AMU graduates.

- **Sir Syed Day (17 October):** The university's Founder's Day is celebrated annually with events, lectures, and gatherings of alumni.
