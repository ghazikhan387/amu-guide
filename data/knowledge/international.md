## International Student Admissions

Aligarh Muslim University welcomes international students from across the globe. The university has a significant international student population from countries in Asia, Africa, the Middle East, and beyond.

## Admission Process for International Students

### Eligibility
- International students must hold qualifications equivalent to the Indian 10+2 (for undergraduate) or bachelor's degree (for postgraduate) from a recognised institution.
- Equivalence of qualifications is determined by the Association of Indian Universities (AIU).
- English proficiency is required as the medium of instruction is English for most programs.

### Application Procedure
1. International students apply through the **AMU International Admissions Office**.
2. Download the application form from the AMU website or contact the International Students' Adviser.
3. Submit completed application with attested copies of:
   - Educational certificates and transcripts
   - Valid passport copy
   - Passport-size photographs
   - English proficiency certificate (if applicable)
   - Medical fitness certificate
4. Some programs may require an entrance test or interview.
5. Admission offers are communicated via email/post.

### Admission Categories
- **Self-Finance Scheme:** International students can apply under the self-financing category with separate fee structure.
- **ICCR Scholarship:** Students sponsored by the Indian Council for Cultural Relations (ICCR) through scholarships offered by Indian embassies abroad.
- **Government-to-Government Agreement:** Students from countries with bilateral education agreements with India.
- **Direct Admission:** Based on merit and equivalence of qualifications.

## Fee Structure for International Students

International students (self-financing) pay higher fees compared to Indian students:
- **Undergraduate Programs:** Approximately $1,000–$3,000 per year
- **Postgraduate Programs:** Approximately $1,500–$4,000 per year
- **Medical Programs (MBBS):** Approximately $3,000–$6,000 per year
- **Engineering Programs (B.Tech.):** Approximately $2,000–$4,000 per year

*ICCR scholarship students are exempt from tuition fees and may receive a monthly stipend.*

## Visa Requirements

- International students need a **Student Visa (S Visa)** to study in India.
- Apply at the nearest Indian Embassy or Consulate with admission letter from AMU.
- Required documents: valid passport, admission letter, financial proof, medical certificate.
- Student visa is typically valid for the duration of the course.
- Visa must be registered with the Foreigners Regional Registration Office (FRRO) within 14 days of arrival.

## Support Services

### International Students' Adviser
- AMU has a dedicated International Students' Adviser who assists with:
  - Admission procedures
  - Visa and immigration guidance
  - Accommodation arrangements
  - Cultural orientation
  - Academic counselling

### Accommodation
- International students are provided hostel accommodation on campus.
- Priority allotment in hostels with better facilities.
- Some students may opt for private accommodation near the campus.

### Other Support
- **Airport pickup:** Assistance with arrival logistics
- **Orientation program:** Introduction to university life, campus tour, and cultural briefing
- **Medical insurance:** Students are advised to obtain medical insurance
- **Cultural activities:** International students participate in cultural events and festivals
