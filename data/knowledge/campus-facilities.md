## Campus Facilities

Aligarh Muslim University's campus is a self-contained township spread over approximately 467.6 hectares, offering a wide range of facilities to students, faculty, and staff.

## Maulana Azad Library

The Maulana Azad Library is one of the largest university libraries in Asia and a treasure trove of knowledge.

- **Collection:** Over 15 lakh (1.5 million) books and periodicals
- **Manuscripts:** Over 15,000 rare manuscripts in Arabic, Persian, Urdu, Hindi, and Sanskrit
- **Reading Halls:** Multiple reading halls with seating for thousands of students
- **Digital Library:** Access to e-journals, e-books, and online databases (JSTOR, Scopus, Web of Science, etc.)
- **Operating Hours:** Typically 8 AM to 12 AM (extended hours during exams)
- **Facilities:** Photocopying, book lending, interlibrary loan, reference service

## Health and Medical Facilities

### Jawaharlal Nehru Medical College Hospital
- A 900+ bed teaching hospital providing comprehensive healthcare
- Emergency services available 24/7
- Specialised departments in all major medical and surgical disciplines
- Free or highly subsidised treatment for university students and staff

### University Health Service
- Health centres located across the campus
- Regular health check-ups for students
- Ambulance service available

## Sports Facilities

AMU has excellent sports infrastructure:

- **Cricket Ground:** Full-sized cricket ground with practice nets
- **Football Ground:** Well-maintained football field
- **Hockey Ground:** Astroturf hockey ground
- **Athletics Track:** Standard running track
- **Swimming Pool:** University swimming pool
- **Gymnasium:** Well-equipped gym facilities
- **Tennis Courts:** Multiple tennis courts
- **Badminton Courts:** Indoor and outdoor courts
- **Table Tennis:** Indoor facilities
- **Basketball Courts:** Multiple courts
- **Volleyball Courts:** Outdoor courts
- **Wrestling Arena:** Indoor wrestling facility

The university participates in inter-university tournaments and has produced several national-level athletes.

## Computer and IT Facilities

- **Computer Centre:** Central computing facility with high-speed internet
- **Wi-Fi:** Campus-wide Wi-Fi connectivity
- **Computer Labs:** Each department has dedicated computer labs
- **LAN:** Wired local area network across academic buildings

## Cafeterias and Food

- **University Canteen:** Central canteen serving affordable meals
- **Hall Messes:** Each hostel has its own dining facility
- **Cafeterias:** Multiple cafeterias across campus
- **Kennedy Dhaba:** A popular food stall area near Kennedy House
- **Food Court:** Near the university market area

## University Market (Shopping Complex)

- Located within the campus
- Bookshops, stationery stores, photocopy centres
- Banks and ATMs (SBI, other banks)
- Mobile recharge and electronics shops
- Tailoring and dry-cleaning services

## Places of Worship

- **University Mosque:** A historic mosque within the campus
- **Other Prayer Halls:** Multiple prayer facilities across campus

## Transportation

- **University Bus Service:** Bus routes connecting different parts of the campus and the city
- **Auto-Rickshaws:** Available within and around campus
- **E-Rickshaws:** Electric rickshaws for campus transport
- **Cycling:** Many students use bicycles to navigate the large campus
- **Railway Station:** Aligarh Junction is approximately 3 km from the main gate

## Other Facilities

- **Post Office:** University post office for mail services
- **Banks:** SBI and other banks have branches within the campus
- **Guest House:** University guest house for visitors
- **Telephone Exchange:** University telephone exchange
- **Power Plant:** Own power generation backup
- **Water Treatment:** University water treatment facility
- **Nursery and Schools:** Schools for children of university staff (from nursery to senior secondary)
